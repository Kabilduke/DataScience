## Mean (μ)
> Formula:

$$ μ = \frac{1}{n} \sum_{i=1}^n x_i $$

## Medium
> Formula:

$$ Odd =  \frac{n+1}{2} $$

$$ Even =  \frac{(\frac{n}{2})^{th} + (\frac{n}{2}+1)^{th}}{2} $$

## Variance
> Formula:

$$ σ^2 = \sum \frac{(x_i - μ)^2}{n} $$

## Standard Deviation
> Formula

$$ σ = \sqrt{\frac{1}{n} \sum_{i-1}^n (x_i - \bar{x})^2}$$
